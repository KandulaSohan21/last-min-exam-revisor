## 📖 The Story

Picture this.

It's 2:13 AM.

Your eyes are red.  
Your syllabus is untouched.  
Your friend says:
> "Bro... just study important questions."

But nobody knows what the important questions are.

That's where **Last Min Exam Revisor** walks in like a guy entering a Tarantino diner scene.
